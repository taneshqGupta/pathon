### Execution Status: Accomplished vs. Remaining

**Accomplished Tasks (The Rust/Svelte Foundation):**
* **Dataset & Environment:** A high-speed headless Rust tick-server was built. Domain Randomization generating mazes and rooms natively fulfills the "Dataset" goal.
* **Path Planning & Sensors:** The global Dijkstra (Flow Field) perfectly recalculates downhill trajectories. Local 15x15 FOV grid and 8-ray LiDAR extraction are strictly implemented for the "Detect... in real time" requirement.
* **Live Simulation:** An `axum` WebSocket streams JSON directly to a lightweight Svelte grid visualization without DOM overhead.
* **FFI & Reward Engineering:** The PyO3 bridge is complete (exposing `reset_env` and `step_action`). The step-penalties ($0.01 time, 0.1 prox) and terminal bounds (+100/-50) correctly calculate inside Rust natively, ensuring blisteringly fast Step logic.
